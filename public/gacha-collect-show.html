Who we are

Built by collectors, for the community

Gacha Collect Show started with a simple frustration: Vietnam's card scene was thriving online and in shop corners, but there was nowhere for everyone to meet in person.

We're collectors, players, and shop owners putting on the show we always wanted to attend — a place to chase the pull, find your grail, and meet the people behind the usernames.

Two cities. Every major game. One growing community.
